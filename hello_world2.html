Hello World!

A program to greet the planet

This page attempts to greet everyone on Earth using various
human languages. We are interested in seeing how long our list can become.


We'll start with languages that the Moringa staff could offer without Google:

  • English: Hello World!
  • Swahili: Hujambo ulimwengu!
  • Japanese: Konnichiwa, sekai!
  • French: Bonjour monde!
